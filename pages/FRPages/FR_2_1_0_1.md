\end{array}

# Universal grading

Each particle can be graded as follows: $$ \text{deg}(\mathbf{1}) = \mathbf{1}', \text{deg}(\mathbf{2}) = \mathbf{2}' $$, where the degrees form the group $$ \mathbb{Z}_2 $$ with multiplication table:

$$
\begin{array}{|ll|}
\hline
 \mathbf{1}' & \mathbf{2}' \\
 \mathbf{2}' & \mathbf{1}' \\
\hline
\end{array}
$$
